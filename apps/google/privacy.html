Audio Dharma Privacy Policy

This app is a product of Christopher Minson LLC. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, you may contact me at christopherjayminson@gmail.com.

The app does not require any form of login. Nor does not identify the user or gather any specific personal information. No personal data is gathered.

The app does gather two pieces of anonymous session information however. These are:

  1. Your current IP
  2. The URL of a talk, that you have just played or shared
None of this data is tied to your identity. These two pieces of data are strictly anonymous, and are used to generate community statistics. This anonymous data is retained permanently, and stored in a password-protected firewalled database. This database is closed to all outside access.
