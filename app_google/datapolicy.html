Audio Dharma Data Policy

The app does not require any form of login. Nor does not identify the user or gather any specific personal information.

The app does gather two pieces of anonymous information however. These are:

  1. Your current IP
  2. The URL of a talk, that you have just played or shared
Nothing is tied to your identifty. These two pieces of data are strictly anomyous, and are used to generate community statistics.
